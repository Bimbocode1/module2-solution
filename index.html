Virtual Learning Platforms

Coursera


Coursera is an online education provider popularly known as Massive Open Online Courses or MOOCs. Coursera provides accredited certificates that are recognised by employers.

Udemy


Udemy is an education technology company that provides an online learning and teaching platform for numerous users across the globe. Udemy offers a wide variety of online courses, where users can learn new skills or improve on existing ones.

LinkedIn-Learning


LinkedIn Learning is an American online learning provider. It provides video courses taught by industry experts in software, creative, and business skills. It is a subsidiary of LinkedIn. LinkedIn Learning is an educational platform that helps you discover and develop your various career skills.
